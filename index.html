About Valborg

Valborg is one of Sweden’s most iconic traditions — a celebration
of spring, community, and new beginnings. At Jönköping
University, it brings together students, friends, and visitors for an
unforgettable day filled with music, energy, and atmosphere.

Set in Hälsoparken, the event blends live performances, great
vibes, and a vibrant crowd to mark the arrival of brighter days.
Whether you're here for the music, the people, or the experience,
Valborg is where the university comes alive.
